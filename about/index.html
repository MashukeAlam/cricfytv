About Cricfy TV

We’re passionate about bringing the best cricket streaming experience to millions of fans worldwide, completely free and accessible to everyone.

Our Mission

At Cricfy TV, we believe that every cricket fan deserves access to high-quality live streaming without the burden of expensive subscriptions or intrusive advertisements. Our mission is to democratize cricket streaming by providing a free, reliable, and user-friendly platform that brings the excitement of cricket directly to your mobile device.

Fan-First Approach

Every decision we make is centered around enhancing the fan experience. We listen to our community and continuously improve based on their feedback.

Innovation

We constantly push the boundaries of mobile streaming technology to deliver faster, smoother, and more reliable cricket streaming experiences.

Privacy & Security

Your privacy matters to us. We implement robust security measures and built-in VPN technology to protect your data and ensure safe streaming.

Our Story

Cricfy TV was born from a simple frustration: cricket fans were being forced to choose between expensive streaming services or unreliable, ad-heavy alternatives. Our founding team of cricket enthusiasts and technology experts came together with a vision to change this landscape.

What started as a small project has grown into a global platform serving millions of cricket fans across the world. From our humble beginnings to reaching version 5.6, we’ve consistently focused on delivering the best possible streaming experience while keeping our service completely free.

Today, Cricfy TV is not just an app – it’s a community of passionate cricket fans who share our belief that great cricket streaming should be accessible to everyone, everywhere.

Our Team

Development Team
